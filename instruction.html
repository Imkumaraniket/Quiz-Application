Dynamic Quiz Instructions

Welcome to the quiz! Here’s everything you need to know before you start

  1. Number of Questions: 5 multiple-choice questions.
  2. Answer Options: Each question has four answer options, but only one is correct.
  3. Navigation: You can use the "Next" and "Previous" buttons to navigate between questions.
  4. Timer: You have 30 seconds to complete the entire quiz.
  5. Auto-submit: The quiz will auto-submit when the time runs out.
  6. Scoring: You'll get 1 point for each correct answer. The total score will be displayed at the end.
  7. Summary: At the end, you'll see a summary of your score, the correct answers, and the time left (if any).
  8. Feedback: For each answer, the system will highlight your choice (green for correct, red for wrong).
  9. Progress: A progress bar will show how many questions you’ve completed.
  10. Restart: After completing the quiz, you can restart it from the summary screen.

Good luck, and have fun!
